What is Sales?
Sales is the act of getting people to buy a product or service.
It is important in business.
Good sales can help a company grow.
It also helps build trust with customers.
Main Idea
Talk clearly
Listen to the customer
Explain why the product helps
Be confident and respectful

Tip 1

Always be confident when speaking to a customer.

Tip 2

Ask questions so you can understand what the customer needs.

Tip 3

Show the value of what you are selling instead of forcing it.
